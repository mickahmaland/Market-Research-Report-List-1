<p><strong>The Benchtop Water Analysis Instrumentation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Assay</li><li>Chemical Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop water analysis instrumentation market is categorized into two main types: physical assays and chemical assays. Physical assays focus on measuring parameters such as turbidity, conductivity, and temperature to assess water quality based on its physical properties. In contrast, chemical assays involve the analysis of specific contaminants and chemical compositions, including pH levels, dissolved oxygen, and nutrient concentrations. Both types are essential for ensuring water safety and compliance with environmental standards, catering to industries such as environmental monitoring, healthcare, and water treatment.</p></p>
