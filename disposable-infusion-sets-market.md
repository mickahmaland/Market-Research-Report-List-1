<p><p>The disposable infusion sets market is categorized into two primary types: pump infusion sets and non-pump infusion sets. Pump infusion sets are designed for use with infusion pumps, providing precise control of medication delivery and allowing for continuous or variable infusion rates. In contrast, non-pump infusion sets are used for gravity or manual administration of fluids and medications, without the aid of a mechanical pump. Both types are essential in medical settings for safe and efficient patient care.</p></p>
