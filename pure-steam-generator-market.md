<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pure Steam Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Re-boiler Generator</li><li>Thermosyphon Generator/ External Rising Film Evaporator</li><li>Falling Film Evaporator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pure Steam Generator market includes various types tailored for different applications. The Re-boiler Generator uses heat exchangers to produce steam by reheating water, ideal for large-scale industrial processes. The Thermosyphon Generator, or External Rising Film Evaporator, employs natural circulation for efficient steam generation, perfect for high-purity demands. The Falling Film Evaporator uses gravity to create a film of liquid on a heated surface, optimizing evaporative efficiency while minimizing energy consumption, making it suitable for pharmaceutical and food industries.</p></p>
